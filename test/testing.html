Discovery of bird species

As of now there are more than 11,000 species of birds that have been discovered across the globe. As such, the tropic is a location home to various unique species such as the Golden bowerbird and Bridled honeyeater.

What do they eat?

In the vast majority of species, birds can contain a maintain a balanced diet; 90% eating insects and nectar, seed or fruit. However, in the case of carnivorous species; such as vultures, diets may

primarily consist of remnants of deceased rodents, small mammals, reptiles etc.

Where to find them!

As you are probably aware, birds are prominent in everywhere across the globe. As such, you can even find specific species of birds at locations such as conservation parks; where they are protected and cared for.

However, to recommend a location in the natural environment, the wetlands would be a great choice to go birdwatching. You could even opt in for a tour guide, another great exception if you really want!
